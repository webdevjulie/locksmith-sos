Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency? expand_more

Our locksmiths are on call 24/7 and typically arrive within 15–30 minutes, depending on your location.

Are your locksmiths certified and insured? expand_more

Yes — all of our locksmiths are bonded, insured, and ALOA certified to provide professional and reliable service.

Can you handle both residential and commercial services? expand_more

Absolutely. We specialize in residential lockouts, lock installations, key duplication, as well as advanced commercial security solutions.

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and secure mobile payments for your convenience.
